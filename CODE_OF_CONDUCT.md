# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Give actionable feedback
- Focus on ideas, not people

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discrimination
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission

## Reporting

Report violations to: security@cascade.fyi

All reports will be reviewed and handled in good faith.
